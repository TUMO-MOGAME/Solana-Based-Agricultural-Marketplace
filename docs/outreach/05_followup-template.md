# Follow-up email template

Send within 24 hours of the meeting. Confirms what was agreed, keeps momentum, and gives them a paper trail.

---

**Subject:** Vuna × [Insurer] — follow-up to our call on [date]

---

Dear [Recipient first name],

Thank you for taking the time to speak with us yesterday / today. I appreciated your candour, especially around **[one specific thing they said that was useful]**.

To recap what we agreed:

1. **[Agreed next step #1, with date.]**
2. **[Agreed next step #2, with date — if any.]**
3. **[The internal contact you said we should reach out to: name and email.]**

I will follow up with **[deliverable]** by **[date]**, and have copied my co-founder so we both stay across the conversation.

A couple of things you mentioned that I want to come back to in our next conversation:

- [Question or detail you flagged but didn't resolve]
- [Data they asked for, or you said you'd think about]

Thank you again. I'll be in touch on [date].

Kind regards,

**Tumo Mogame**
Co-founder, Project Vuna
[Email] · [Phone]

---

## Notes

- **Always cc your co-founder.** Continuity matters more than tidy inboxes.
- **Be specific about dates.** "Next week" is dead air. "Tuesday 14 May" is a commitment.
- **Don't ask new big questions in the follow-up.** Save those for the next call.
- **If they raised a concern in the meeting, address it directly here.** Don't pretend it didn't happen.
