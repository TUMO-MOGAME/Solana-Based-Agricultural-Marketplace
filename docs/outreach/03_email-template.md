# Cover-email template

Use this as a starting point. Customise the **bracketed sections** per insurer using the angle from `01_insurer-targets.md`.

> **Tone check:** professional, brief, respectful of their time. No hype, no jargon, no "blockchain", no "revolutionary", no exclamation marks. Read it aloud before sending — if it sounds like a startup pitch, rewrite.

---

**Subject:** Underwriting partnership exploration — smallholder parametric crop insurance

---

Dear [Recipient first name],

I hope this finds you well. My name is **Tumo Mogame**, and I am building **Project Vuna** — a digital platform aimed at bringing credit, certified inputs, and parametric drought insurance to South African smallholder farmers, beginning with maize in the Eastern Cape.

We are not seeking to underwrite the insurance ourselves. Under the FAIS Act and the Insurance Act 2017, that requires a licensed underwriter, which is why I am writing to you. **[Insurer name]** stands out to us because **[customised reason — see 01_insurer-targets.md for the angle]**.

Vuna's model is to handle the digital distribution, cooperative-mediated farmer onboarding, and automated payout execution, while a licensed insurer underwrites the parametric crop policy. The platform is mobile-first and designed to operate at a price point that smallholder farmers can afford — something the broker channel cannot economically reach.

I am not asking for a commitment of any kind. I would simply value 30 minutes of your time in the next two weeks for an introductory conversation, and to learn what a partnership of this kind would look like from your side.

I have attached a one-page summary. I would be happy to share our full product and regulatory documentation if useful.

Thank you for considering this. I look forward to hearing from you.

With kind regards,

**Tumo Mogame**
Co-founder, Project Vuna
[Email] · [Phone] · [LinkedIn]

## Sending notes

- **CC** the co-founder so the thread stays visible to both of you.
- Send Tuesday-Thursday morning (best open rates for SA business email).
- If no reply in 7 working days, send one polite follow-up. Never more than two follow-ups per contact.
- Track in `01_insurer-targets.md`'s contact log.
