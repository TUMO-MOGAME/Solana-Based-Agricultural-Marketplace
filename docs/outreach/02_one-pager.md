## Project Vuna

**A digital platform that brings credit, certified inputs, and parametric crop insurance to South African smallholder farmers — bundled into one Grow Pack, repaid at harvest.**

### The problem

Smallholders produce roughly 70% of Sub-Saharan Africa's food but receive less than 5% of commercial bank lending. They cannot access certified inputs, they cannot insure against drought, and one failed season can wipe out a family. Crop-insurance penetration in Sub-Saharan Africa is below 3%.

### The product

A mobile platform delivered through farming cooperatives. A registered farmer applies for a Grow Pack — a pre-configured bundle of certified seeds, fertilizer, and a parametric drought policy. The cooperative reviews and approves. Suppliers deliver inputs directly. At harvest, the cost is auto-deducted from sale proceeds. Each successful season builds a portable credit history that follows the farmer.

### Why we are reaching out

We do not seek to underwrite insurance. We seek a **licensed underwriting partner** who can issue the parametric crop policy while we provide:

- Digital distribution into the smallholder market
- Cooperative-mediated KYC and on-the-ground trust
- Real-time data ingestion for parametric triggers
- Automated, transparent payout execution
- POPIA-compliant farmer data handling

### What we offer the underwriter

- A distribution channel into a smallholder market that is uneconomic to serve through traditional brokers
- Lower per-policy operating cost via automated payouts
- Auditable, real-time policy and claims data
- Public-impact alignment for development-finance and ESG reporting

### Where we are

Hackathon-stage MVP for the Solana 2026 Frontier Hackathon. We have a complete product proposal, regulatory analysis (NCA, FAIS, FSCA, SARB, POPIA), and design mockups. We have not deployed real money. We are reaching out to a small number of South African agri-insurers before committing to an architecture.

### Regulatory awareness

We understand that under the FAIS Act and the Insurance Act 2017, the parametric product must be underwritten by a licensed FSP and insurer. That is precisely why we are seeking a partner and not building this alone.
